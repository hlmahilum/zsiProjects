Competitive Strengths 

• Establish Global Network and Strong Customer Relationships.
Circuits Plus Group has a strategic network of sales agencies worldwide to serve its geographical spread of customers. The Group has also developed strong working relationships with its clients in a wide spectrum of industries.

• Manufacturing Expertise
More than 30 years of experience (since 1981) in manufacturing PCBs and constant investment in machinery, training and technologies development has given the Group strategic advantage in providing better service to its customers and high quality PCBs.

• Accessibility to Technological Development
The Group participates actively in product development programmes to enhance its product quality and process capability. In addition, the Group works closely with its material and equipment suppliers to provide total PCB solutions to its customers.

• Product Quality
Circuits Plus and CPM were awarded ISO 9001 & ISO 14001 certifications for their commitment to quality products and services, as well as to environmental preservation.

• Strong Management Team
Our Group has a pool of highly experienced and well qualified management staff, supported by trained technical personnel, to meet the increasing demands and challenges of the global market.
