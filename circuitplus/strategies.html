Strategies


The Group’s growth strategies in relation to its PCB business are to:
1. Build a diversified portfolio of customers and strengthen its global network.
2. Improve production flexibility and efficiency.
3. Upgrade the Group’s technical capability to keep abreast of competition.
4. Produce higher-end products with higher yield margins.
5. Optimize regional operations to take advantage of lower costs.
6. Continue to invest in Technologies Development.
