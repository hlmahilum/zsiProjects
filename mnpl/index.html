Company Introduction

Manufacturing Network Pte Ltd (“MNPL”) was established in November 2000 in Singapore. Our main business includes the stocking, distribution and cutting of aluminium alloy plates, sheets and extrusion bars and profiles. Our aluminium alloy products are sold to and used in many industries including precision engineering, transport engineering, semiconductor equipment, electronics, and many others.

In addition to our warehouse and cutting facilities in Singapore, MNPL group has warehouses with cutting facilities in Malaysia (MNPL Aluminium Centre Sdn. Bhd.) and China (MNPL Metals Co., Ltd.) as well. With our wide network of locations, MNPL group is able to cater to the regional needs of our valuable customers.

MNPL group strives to create value for our customers. Quality and service are our most important objectives.
