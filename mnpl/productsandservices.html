Our Products and Services

Our principal activities are the import and export of non-ferrous metals; and the operation of metal service centres, specializing in aluminium alloys (2024, 5052, 5083, 6061, 7075, MIC-6 plates, sheets, rods and bars). MNPL holds an inventory of about 1,000 metric tons in our factory which is available to our customers who wish to cut to size and to close tolerances. As a metal service centre, MNPL acquire products from primary producers and then process the aluminium alloys to meet customer specifications, including specified lengths, widths, shapes and surface characteristics. Through this one-stop metal service centre, this process saves time, labour and expense for customers, thereby reducing their overall manufacturing costs. MNPL has a huge supply network chain worldwide and an enterprising and resourceful management team. Coupled with strong relationships with our international suppliers and our continuous strive for excellence, MNPL is able to provide high quality products and excellent services to our customers.

MNPL strives to increase productivity and reduce manufacturing/ fabrication costs for our customers by providing high quality and excellent machineability materials for the most demanding manufacturing and process applications. Our products possess excellent qualities (surface finish, resistance to deformity, close tolerance, uniformity, machineability) and maintains its flatness, even after being subjected to many cutting and machining process. No minimum order quantity is required.
