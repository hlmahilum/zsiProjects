About me

Some things just look better in motion and in the highly competitive world of fashion, finding an edge over the competition is crucial. When it comes to clothes, hair, make-up and jewellery nothing compares to seeing moving images. So why not give your audience what they prefer?

Our video bay features an 8m fashion catwalk, Kino Flo lighting, two BBC approved video cameras, backdrop for the addition of brand logos if required and changing/makeup facilities, perfect for online fashion video.
